Our Story
Pathivara group had its initial entrance in the business world in the year with its first venture of. Thereafter the group made has stayed committed to be at the top of the heap and make its mark in all projects undertaken. Pathivara group’s success over the last years has been a pioneer of diversification and growth in the horizon of eastern Nepal. The group, with its incredible team of diligent and ingenious professionals, has been tirelessly working to ensure quality and customer satisfaction in all of our products and services. Since, we have expanded our business portfolio by making a strong presence in Construction and Manufacturing industries, Educational Institutions, Hospitality Services, Interior and Furnishing, Consumer Goods distribution, Fuel Retailing, Logistic Services, Agricultural Industry and Automotive Industry.


Message From Chairman


This is my pleasure to introduce you to ‘Pathivara group’ – a rapidly growing business conglomerate of Nepal. Since the time I started my first business until becoming the chairman of Pathivara group, I have believed in hard work and patience. When I transcended from nothing to something in this business journey, I learnt a number of valuable life lessons. However, for me ‘Humility’ has been the most important attribute that I learnt working for these many years. It's important to remain rooted even in the highest points of life. Over the years, our group has been loyal and committed to its core values. We strive to maintain the highest standards in whatever we do. We believe in quality and building long term relationships with our trading partners and customers. In addition to enhancements in our business growth prospects, we also seek to realize our potential to fulfill our social, moral and environmental duties. I appreciate my colleagues for their dedication and commitment towards the company. I am grateful to every employee associated with us, working tirelessly, to make ‘Pathivara’ a name synonymous to excellence. I also want to thank all our business partners, customers, trading partners and financial partners for their tacit and unshakable belief over the years. Lastly, I would like to give a message to everyone that this is just the beginning of the many milestones, that we are yet to reach.
