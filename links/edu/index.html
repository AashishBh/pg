Education

Pathivara group believes in the principle, “leaders create leaders” and with this objective in mind we decided to build young leaders by establishing educational institutions in our province. Our first institution was NEPAL DVM GLOBAL ACADEMY Pvt. Ltd. Established on providing higher secondary level education to students. Since then we have diversified and expanded our horizons to enter into other varied areas of educational sector like health education, business education etc. Through our various institutes we provide a number of courses to help Nepalese youth prepare for building their own as well as the nation’s future.


People’s Multiple Health Institute Pvt. Ltd.


People’s Multiple Health Institute was established in 2003 AD (2060 BS). Located in Itahari, Sunsari district of Nepal, it offers PCL Nursing (Staff Nurse) programs. It is affiliated to Council for Technical Education (CTEVT) and has been recognized by Nepal Nursing Council (NNC). The main goal of this institute is to produce capable nursing man power for the nation.


Kasturi Academy Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2055 BS, Kasturi Academy is one of the leading academic institutions of Nepal for the quality education. It offers education starting from Pre-Primary level to Master’s level.


Kasturi College of Management


Kasturi College of Management offers quality education exclusively in Management stream. It is one of the leading academic institutions of Nepal. It offers Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) and Master of Business Studies (MBS) courses in affiliation from Purbanchal University.

Nepal DVM school was constructed with a vision to provide wholesome knowledge to children.
