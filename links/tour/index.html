Tourism and Hospitality
Nepal famous for its snowcapped mountains, abundant flora and fauna, exciting trekking routes and rich cultural and religious diversity has provided investors a vast array of opportunities to explore its inexhaustible potential and Pathivara group since, its beginning has worked with a simple formula to use resources at hand in the most efficient way to pave the path for successful entrepreneurial enterprises. In line with this, we decided to enter into Tourism and Hospitality industry to find our place and ultimately make our mark in the sector.
At present, we have set up two well-constructed facilities for hospitality services and one amusement park which can easily be labelled as a tourist landmark for Eastern Nepal.


Wet and Dino Amusement and Water Park Pvt. Ltd., Itahari


Situated in and spread over an area of Wet and Dino Amusement and Water Park is believed to become one of the most popular point of attraction for tourists as well as the locals. This water park has been designed keeping ‘fun’ as the major priority. With multiple Swimming Pools, numerous exciting water Slides, unmissable thrilling experiences, 3 Restaurants within the premise to serve drool worthy food and much more, you will be bound to stay an extra hour in the park.
For people who want to add some thrill to your vacation or for people who just want to lay near the pool under the sun, this water park is a one-stop solution for all your relaxation needs.


Hotel Garima Pvt. Ltd., Itahari


Located in the outskirts of Itahari, ‘Garima’ is a 5- star hotel with 92 rooms. The location makes it perfect as it is a little away from city chaos and but still close enough for you to reach your desired destination in the city. With excellent services and amenities Garima hotel is the perfect stay for anyone travelling to or near Itahari.


Nepalirika Hotel Pvt. Ltd. (Damak)


Located in the heart of Damak city Nepalirika is a 3- star hotel with 55 rooms. It’s a one stop solution for all your accommodation needs. It’s one of the most popular hotels amongst the corporate travelers.
